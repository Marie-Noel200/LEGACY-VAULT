Inactivity Threshold

Set your total inactivity period. The system divides it into 3 equal checkpoints and sends you a "are you still active?" email at each one.

e.g. 150 days → Check 1 at day 50, Check 2 at day 100, Check 3 at day 150
Min: 30 days · Max: 730 days · Checkpoints at ÷3 intervals
How It Works
1
Check 1 — Day ⅓
First "are you still active?" email sent. Click confirm → timer resets.
2
Check 2 — Day ⅔
Second check if you ignored Check 1. Still time to confirm.
3
Check 3 — Final Day
Final warning. Confirm now or emergency will be triggered.
!
Emergency Triggered
All 3 checks ignored → trusted contacts notified with codes.
✓
2-of-3 Approval
2 out of 3 contacts approve → vault access granted.
